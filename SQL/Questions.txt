#Stratscratch problems
1.Summarize the activity of each customer by calculating their total number of interactions and content items created.
  
  Your output should include the customer's ID, the total number of interactions, and the total number of content items.
  Tables: customer_interactions, user_content
  Table 1 Name: customer_interactions 
  Columns in customer_interactions: interaction_id:int, customer_id:int, interaction_type:varchar, interaction_date:datetime
  Table 2 Name: user_content
  Columns in user_content: content_id:int, customer_id:int, content_type:varchar, content_text:varchar

Solution:
  Select 
    ci.customer_id,
    count(Distinct ci.interaction_id), 
    count(Distinct u.content_id) 
  from customer_interactions ci Left join user_content u 
  on ci.customer_id=u.customer_id 
  group by ci.customer_id;
